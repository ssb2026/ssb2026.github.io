Systematic Biology Editor Meet and Greet

Led by Bob Thomson

NOTE: This will be held over lunch on Saturday, Jan. 10th

An informal opportunity for early career researchers to interact with members of our flagship journal's editorial team. Participants will discuss with editors how to publish in Systematic Biology, what makes a study a good fit for the journal, and how editorial decisions get made. Other potential topics include the changing academic publishing landscape, the role of society-owned journals, how to get involved with the journal as a reviewer or associate editor, along with time for open Q&A.
